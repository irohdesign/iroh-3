Who Are You?

I'm Jalia Evans, a Junior Front End Developer based in Washington DC. I am passionate about User Interface Design, content-aware development, and animation.

I am self-taught, with the aid of the internet. I am currently open to new projects, and excited to see what's out there.

What Do You Do?

I build websites and applications that are responsive, efficient, and clean. I work with both small and large clients, and can accommodate a wide range of goals.

I use an agile design method to ensure my projects fulfill their purpose. I build my websites from scratch, and only use skeleton files when constructing Wordpress themes. To see specific skills, visit my About page.

Who's Iroh?

The name comes from Avatar the Last Airbender, a show I watched growing up with my brother. General Iroh was a kind and wise, yet exceedingly unusual, man who advocated for balance above all else.
